About

This web application has been created in order to automate scheduling of pantry volunteers.

Instructions:

First, download the CSVs for the volunteer availabilities and the pantry schedule from the Airtable database.

Next, upload both files to the web application.

Then, click submit to have the automated schedule donwload directly to your browser.

Finally, upload the CSV back to the Airtable database for easy readabilitiy.
